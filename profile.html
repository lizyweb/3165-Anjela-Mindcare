This is Dr. Anjela With over 15 years of experience in the field of psychology, I am a passionate advocate for mental wellness and personal growth. As a seasoned psychologist with a Ph.D., I specialize in a range of therapeutic modalities aimed at fostering holistic well-being and empowering individuals to lead fulfilling lives.

Services Offered :

• Individual Psychotherapy : Tailored sessions to address personal challenges and facilitate self-discovery.

• Cognitive Behavioral Therapy : Harnessing the power of thoughts and behaviors to effect positive change.

• Emotional Behavior Therapy : Nurturing emotional intelligence and resilience.

• Mindfulness & Hypnosis Therapy : Techniques for deep relaxation and heightened awareness.

• Relationship & Couple Therapy : Strengthening bonds and fostering healthier connections.

• Acceptance & Commitment Therapy : Embracing values and fostering psychological flexibility.

• Sex & Physical Intimacy Therapy : Safe spaces for exploring intimacy and enhancing relationships.

• Art Therapy : Unlocking creativity as a therapeutic tool for self-expression.

• Play Therapy : Engaging techniques to support children in overcoming challenges.

• Music & Dance Therapy : Harnessing the power of rhythm and movement for emotional healing.

• Imaginative Reflective Therapy : Tapping into the subconscious to explore inner landscapes.

• Sleep & Relaxation Therapy : Techniques for achieving restful sleep and deep relaxation.

Special Expertise :

• Brain Profiling : Understanding individual cognitive strengths and areas for development.

• Prenatal Women's Health Care : Supporting expectant mothers in navigating the emotional journey of pregnancy.

• Physical Wellness : Integrating mental and physical health for overall well-being.

• Mental Wellness : Holistic approaches to mental health maintenance and recovery.

• Addiction : Comprehensive support for individuals on the path to recovery.

Services Include :

• Counseling : Personalized guidance and support through life's challenges.

• Therapies : Evidence-based interventions tailored to individual needs.

• Detox & Inpatient Care : Safe and supportive environments for detoxification and recovery.

• Aftercare & Follow-Up : Continued support and guidance post-treatment.

• Relapse Prevention : Strategies for maintaining long-term well-being and resilience.

I am here to support you on your journey towards a happier, healthier life.
