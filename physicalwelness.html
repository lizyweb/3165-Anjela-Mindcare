Physical Wellness

1. WEIGHT LOSS AND Obesity

We provide Common treatments for overweight and obesity include losing weight through healthy eating, being more physically active, and making other changes to your usual habits. Weight-management programs may help some people lose weight or keep from regaining lost weight. The key to losing weight is paying attention to the right combination of three components: good nutrition, physical ctivity and behavior modification.
These include diet, lack of exercise, environmental factors, and genetics Regular exercise is vital for both physical and mental health. Increasing the frequency of physical activity in a disciplined and purposeful way is often crucial for successful weight loss. One hour of moderate-intensity activity per day, such as brisk walking, is ideal.

3. Eating Disorders

Eating Disorders are:

o Eating disorders are behavioral conditions characterized by severe and persistent disturbance in eating behaviors and associated distressing thoughts and emotions. They can be very serious conditions affecting physical, psychological and social function.
o Frequent dieting, anxiety associated with specific foods or meal skipping.
o Chronic weight fluctuations.
o Rigid rituals and routines surrounding food and exercise.
o Feelings of guilt and shame associated with eating

4. Sleep Disorders

Some of the signs and symptoms of sleep disorders include excessive daytime sleepiness, irregular breathing or increased movement during sleep. Other signs and symptoms include an irregular sleep and wake cycle and difficulty falling asleep

Thankfully, there are treatments available that you can talk to your patients about for the five most common sleep disorders:

• Insomnia.
• Sleep Apnea.
• Narcolepsy.
• Restless Legs Syndrome.
• REM sleep Behavior Disorder.
